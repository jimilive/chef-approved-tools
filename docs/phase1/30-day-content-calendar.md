## Content Creation Workflow

### Daily Routine (4-5 hours per post)

**Hour 1: Research & Outline**
- Keyword research (Google Trends, AnswerThePublic, Amazon autocomplete)
- Analyze top 10 Google results for target keyword
- Note gaps in competitor content
- Create detailed outline from template

**Hours 2-3: Writing**
- Write 1500-3000 words
- Insert affiliate links every 2-3 paragraphs
- Follow template structure
- Personal experience and testing details
- Comparison tables where relevant

**Hour 4: SEO & Optimization**
- Optimize title & meta description
- Add schema markup (Product, Review, FAQ, Breadcrumb)
- Internal linking to related posts
- Alt text for all images
- Check keyword density and readability

**Hour 5: Publishing & Promotion**
- Final proofread
- Compress and upload images
- Push to GitHub
- Auto-deploy via Netlify
- Submit URL to Google Search Console
- Share on social media
- Create Pinterest pin
